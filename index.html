About AI Summit 2026

AI Summit is a state-level technical symposium where UG and PG engineering students innovate, compete, and lead the future in AI, ML, IoT, Robotics, and emerging technologies, featuring flagship events like Paper Presentation, Poster Presentation, and Startup Expo.

Key Takeaways

  • Exposure to latest AI trends & applications
  • Enhance technical presentation skills
  • Build innovation & entrepreneurial thinking
  • Network with experts & peers
  • Win from ₹1 Lakh prize pool
  • Participation certificates for all
