Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in AI Summit 2026?
Only UG & PG Engineering students (any year, any branch) from recognized colleges across India.
How do I register?
Scan the QR code above or click the Register button → Choose your event → Pay ₹200 per event on KonfHub.
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes! You can register and participate in all three events by paying ₹200 per event separately.
Is the registration fee refundable?
No, registration fees are non-refundable except in case of event cancellation by organizers.
What should I bring on the event day?
College ID card, printed copies (for paper/poster), laptop/prototype (for expo), and AI Summit T-shirt (mandatory dress code).
Will certificates be provided?
Yes! Participation certificate for all, Merit certificates for winners, and Excellence certificates for consolation prize winners.
